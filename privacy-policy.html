SubKill - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

1. Introduction

SubKill ("we", "our", "the app") is an Android application for tracking personal subscriptions. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how your data is handled.

2. Data We Collect

Subscription data you enter: Names, prices, currencies, billing cycles, renewal dates, categories, and notes for your subscriptions. This data is stored locally on your device only using a local SQLite database. We do not have access to this data and it is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Purchase information: If you purchase SubKill Premium, the transaction is processed entirely by Google Play. We receive a purchase token to verify your premium status. We do not collect your payment details (credit card number, billing address, etc.).

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for advertising purposes
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Bank account or financial institution data

3. How We Use Your Data

All subscription data you enter is used solely to provide you with the app's core functionality: tracking subscriptions, calculating totals, sending renewal reminders, and displaying charts. This data never leaves your device.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your Android device using Android's Room database (SQLite). The data is protected by your device's security measures (screen lock, encryption). We do not operate any servers that store your data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. The only external service the app communicates with is Google Play Billing for processing premium purchases.

6. Third-Party Services

Google Play Billing: Used for processing in-app purchases. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not integrate any advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, or social media trackers.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), if you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access: All your data is visible directly in the app.
  • Right to rectification: You can edit any subscription data at any time.
  • Right to erasure: You can delete individual subscriptions or use "Delete All Data" in Settings to permanently remove all data.
  • Right to data portability: CSV export functionality is available (Premium feature).
  • Right to object: Since we do not process your data on servers, this right is inherently fulfilled.

8. Data Retention

Your data persists on your device until you delete it manually (via the app or by uninstalling). We have no server-side data to retain.

9. Children's Privacy

SubKill is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Notifications

SubKill sends local notifications to remind you of upcoming subscription renewals. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers. You can disable notifications at any time via Android system settings.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
